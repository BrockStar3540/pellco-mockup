04Leadership

The name on the door.

Founder · President

Mike Pellitteri

Founder · President · Principal-in-Charge

Mike founded Pellco in 1997 with one excavator, one dump truck, and a reputation built one handshake at a time. Twenty-seven years later, he's still the name on the door — and still the one you'll meet in the trailer when the stakes are high.

Under his direction, Pellco has grown from a two-person crew to a $15M-bonded general contractor trusted by school districts, municipalities, utility districts, and private developers across the Pacific Northwest. What hasn't changed: the principal is always accountable for the work that carries his family name.

— Mike Pellitteri, President
